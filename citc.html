CAUGHT IN THE CROSSFIRE

The best contradications to one's points are made by himself .

ABOUT

Are you good at making a point? Good! But that's too mainstream!
Let's see if you can contradict what you say too!

RULES

  • Maximum of 2 members per team are eligible to participate.
  • Consist of 2 rounds .
  • Brainstorm your way to the finals where "bouncing and pouncing" is waiting for you.
  • Usage of electronic gadgets is strictly prohibited.
  • No time extension will be provided for each round.
  • The decision of the judge will be final and will not be subject to any change.

TASK

  • Individual event
  • The first round will be an extempo speech round where students have to express their opinions on a give topic while following the constraints given on the spot.
  • The best speakers will progress to the second round.
  • The second round will be a block and tackle round and students will have to speak for and against a topic according to the judges' command of block and tackle respectively.
  • Humour and creativity will be much appreciated. any form of direct vulgarity will lead to immediate disqualification.

FAQs

Who can participate ?
Student from any college or university with a valid ID card can participate in the competition.

How to register ?
Registration is only by online .
How many persons can be there in one team?
One team can have maximum of 2 members.

Can I register in more than one competition?
Yes, you can participate in more than one competition. It is recommended to focus on one competition as there may be some chances of slot clash.
